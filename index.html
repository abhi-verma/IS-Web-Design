Welcome to GoCamping

GoCamping is one of the leading companies that helps you search, book, and explore hundreds of campsites, campgrounds, farms and more, across the U.S. Our mission is to make the process of finding a place to camp easy for our camping enthusiasts and newbies who want to explore new places in the wild.

Our company is headquartered in Austin, Texas with an employee strength of 25. Our founder and CEO Shaun Kapoor, stumbled upon this idea when he and his best buds from college were looking for campsites for their Spring Break. They found it extremely difficult to find a single, comprehensive source of information for all their camping needs. For instance, they did not know what to expect when they reached a campground, the equipment required and so on. And thatâ€™s how the idea of GoCamping was born.
